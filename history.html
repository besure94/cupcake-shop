History

Here at Outsider Cupcakes, we strive to deliver you delicious, creative cupcakes that you won't find anywhere else!

Founded in 1970, our owner Venus Anderson, was growing tired of the lack of quality deserts in the city of Portland, OR. She decided to combine her love of baking and outer space into one! Shortly afterwards, Outsider Cupcakes was born. Our first store opened in the Kenton neighborhood and we have since opened stores in NW Portland, SE Portland, and another in Eugene, OR! We plan to capitalize on our success and open stores in other Pacific Northwest cities as soon as possible.
